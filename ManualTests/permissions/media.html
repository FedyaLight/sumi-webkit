Camera And Microphone

Browser UI Expectations

  • First camera, microphone, or combined request shows the URL-bar authorization prompt.
  • Allowed capture keeps the URL-bar active permission indicator visible while tracks are active or muted.
  • URL hub -> Permissions shows Camera and Microphone rows plus Active now runtime controls.
  • Privacy -> Site Settings lists saved Camera and Microphone decisions only when a saved site decision exists.
  • macOS camera or microphone denial shows system-blocked UI and must not store a site Block.
